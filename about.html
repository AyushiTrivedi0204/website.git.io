ABOUT OUR DISH

Gulab jamun (also spelled gulaab jamun) is a milk-solid-based sweet from the Indian subcontinentGulab jamun was first prepared in medieval India and was derived from a fritter that Central Asian Turkic invaders brought to India, Other theory claims that it was accidentally prepared by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan's personal chef.
